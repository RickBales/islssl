On November 7, 2015, the United States Branch sponsored a seminar on Crowd-Sourcing and the Law. The seminar was co-sponsored by the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania.

Presentations were made by:

            David Durward
            Germany

            Antonio Aloisi
            Italy

            Michael Silberman
            Germany

            Valerio de Stefano
            ILO, Switzerland

            Miriam Cherry
            United States

            Jeremias Prassl
            United Kingdom

            Martin Risak
            Austria

            Janine Berg
            ILO, France

The papers will be published in the Comparative Labor Law & Policy Journal.
