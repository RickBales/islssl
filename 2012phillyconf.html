2009 Chicago Conference

On July 2, 2012, the United States Branch sponsored a seminar on Public Sector Bargaining Around the World. Leading scholars from the United States, Japan, France, Canada, Germany, Greece and Italy discussed their public sector unions. Each country was represented by a legal and a non-legal scholar. The conference was organized by Matt Finkin from the University of Illinois.

The seminar was co-sponsored by the Canadian Branch of the International Society for Labour and Social Security Law and it was offered in conjunction with the 16th World Congress of the International Labour and Employment Relations Association.
